About 101 Bagels

Located just minutes from the iconic Pacific Coast Highway, 101 Bagels & Subs in Oceanside, California, has been a local favorite since 1998. What started as a small family-run shop with a passion for crafting authentic New York–style bagels quickly grew into a beloved neighborhood eatery known for its fresh ingredients, warm hospitality, and unbeatable breakfast and lunch options. Every bagel is made fresh daily, boiled and baked to perfection, while our signature subs are piled high with premium meats, cheeses, and crisp veggies. Whether you’re grabbing a quick coffee and bagel on your way to the beach or sitting down for a hearty lunch with friends, 101 Bagels & Subs offers a true taste of Oceanside comfort. Over the years, 101 Bagels & Subs has become more than just a place to eat—it’s a community gathering spot where locals and visitors alike come together to enjoy great food and good vibes. Our team takes pride in serving the Oceanside area with friendly service, quality you can taste, and flavors that keep you coming back. From classic favorites like the Everything Bagel with house-made cream cheese to creative signature subs inspired by California’s laid-back surf culture, we’re proud to carry on a tradition of freshness, flavor, and family right here in North County San Diego.
